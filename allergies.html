Allergy Information for Our Products

Important Allergy Information

Please review the following information before consuming our products. If you have any allergies, please consult with our staff.

Chatpate

A spicy and tangy Indian snack made with potatoes, spices, and a variety of chutneys. Contains: Potatoes, Spices, Tamarind, and various chutneys.

Allergy Warning: May contain traces of nuts and gluten. Not suitable for individuals with nut or gluten allergies.

Panipuri

Popular street food consisting of crispy puris filled with spicy tamarind water, chickpeas, and potatoes. Contains: Wheat (Puris), Chickpeas, Tamarind.

Allergy Warning: Contains gluten. May contain traces of peanuts. Not suitable for individuals with gluten or peanut allergies.

Laphing

A Tibetan dish made of spicy and tangy noodles. Contains: Wheat noodles, Spices, Vinegar.

Allergy Warning: Contains gluten. May also contain traces of sesame seeds. Not suitable for individuals with sesame seed or gluten allergies.
