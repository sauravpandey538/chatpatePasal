About Us

Welcome to HmroChatPat! We are delighted to share our journey with you.

Our Beginning

We started HmroChatPat in early 2020 with a vision to bring authentic and delicious snacks to our community. Our journey began with a small kitchen and a lot of passion for cooking.

First Outlet

In July 2021, we opened our first outlet in Bhaktapur . The grand opening was a huge success, and we were thrilled to see the positive response from our customers.

Expansion

By the end of 2022, we expanded our menu to include a variety of traditional and modern dishes. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has driven our growth.

Recent Milestones

In 2023, we celebrated our second anniversary with the opening of a new outlet at samakhusi. We continue to innovate and improve to serve you better.

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future, our goal is to further expand our reach and continue providing exceptional food and service. We are excited about the new opportunities and are committed to making every visit memorable.
